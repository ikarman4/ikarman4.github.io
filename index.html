So What Is This?

Follow two very stupid, but curious kids as they navigate and explore big, seemingly intangible  questions all people interested in research struggle with: what differentiates prolific researchers from those around them, are there topics or problems worth spending a lifetime studying, what are the actionable steps that could help elucidate my own passions. Join us in getting to know as well learning valuable lessons from passionate people, leaders that are tasked with solving the ever growing number of problems we face today through research.
