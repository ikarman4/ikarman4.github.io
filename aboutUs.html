About Us

Ian Karman

Ian Karman is a student studying neuroscience and computer science at Boston University. His academic interests are in machine learning, app development, and cognitive and computational neuroscience. Outside of his studies he often finds himself exploring the city of Boston through photography, food, and comedy clubs. After graduation from Boston University in 2022, Ian hopes to continue his academic career in pursuit of graduate opportunities. Post education he hopes to work in the neuroscience research field in brain mapping and interfacing.

Ankur Bamezai

Ankur Bamezai is a sophomore at Boston University studying neuroscience and Entrepreneurship. His academic interests lie in the fields of neuroscience of learning and memory, neuroendocrinology, as well as innovation in the education space. Outside of school he loves hanging out with friends, playing soccer, weightlifting, and meditation. After graduating Boston University in 2022, he hopes to bridge his interests in neuroscience and education in through either cognitive neuroscience research or diving into the Edtech space. He is ultimately aiming to use his interpersonal and analytical skills to contribute to the growing trend of using brain sciences and technology to create greater educational equity.
